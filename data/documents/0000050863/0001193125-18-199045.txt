<TD WIDTH="12%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02.</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">On June&nbsp;21, 2018, Intel Corporation (&#147;Intel&#148;) announced that, on June&nbsp;20, 2018, Brian M. Krzanich resigned as Chief Executive Officer and a member
of Intel&#146;s Board of Directors (the &#147;Board&#148;). On June&nbsp;20, 2018, the Board appointed Robert (&#147;Bob&#148;) H. Swan, 58, Intel&#146;s Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, to serve as interim Chief Executive
Officer, effective immediately. Mr.&nbsp;Swan also continues to serve as CFO of Intel. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;Swan has been Intel&#146;s Executive Vice President, Chief
Financial Officer since joining Intel in October 2016. In that capacity, he has overseen Intel&#146;s global finance organization&#151;including finance, accounting and reporting, tax, treasury, internal audit, and investor relations&#151;IT, and
the Corporate Strategy Office. From September 2015 to September 2016, Mr.&nbsp;Swan served as an Operating Partner at General Atlantic LLC, a private equity firm. He served as Senior Vice President, Finance and Chief Financial Officer of eBay Inc.
from March 2006 to July 2015. Previously, Mr.&nbsp;Swan served as Executive Vice President, Chief Financial Officer of Electronic Data Systems Corporation, Executive Vice President, Chief Financial Officer of TRW Inc., as well as Chief Financial
Officer, Chief Operating Officer, and Chief Executive Officer of Webvan Group, Inc. Mr.&nbsp;Swan began his career in 1985 at General Electric, serving for 15 years in numerous senior finance roles. Mr.&nbsp;Swan also serves as a member of the board
of directors of eBay, Inc. </P>
