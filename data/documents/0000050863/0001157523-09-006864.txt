      SANTA CLARA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--October 13, 2009--Intel
      Corporation today reported third-quarter revenue of $9.4 billion. The
      company reported operating income of $2.6 billion, net income of $1.9
      billion and earnings per share (EPS) of 33 cents.
    </p>
    <p>
      &#8220;Intel&#8217;s strong third-quarter results underscore that computing is
      essential to people&#8217;s lives, proving the importance of technology
      innovation in leading an economic recovery,&#8221; said Paul Otellini, Intel
      president and CEO. &#8220;This momentum in the current economic climate, plus
      our product leadership, gives us confidence about our business prospects
      going forward. As we look ahead, Intel&#8217;s game-changing 32nm process
      technology will usher in another wave of innovation from new, powerful
      Intel&#174; Xeon&#8482; server platforms to high-performance Intel&#174; Core&#8482;
      processors to low-power Intel&#174; Atom&#8482; processors.&#8221;
