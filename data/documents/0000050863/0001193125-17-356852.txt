<TD WIDTH="12%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">On May&nbsp;3, 2017, Intel Corporation (&#147;Intel&#148;) reported that Diane Bryant would cease to serve as head of Intel&#146;s Data Center Group and would take a
personal leave of absence, which was expected to last approximately six to eight months. On November&nbsp;29, 2017, Ms.&nbsp;Bryant notified Intel that she will not be returning to Intel and plans to retire from the company effective as of
December&nbsp;1, 2017. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">In connection with her departure, Intel expects to enter into an agreement with Ms.&nbsp;Bryant pursuant to which Ms.&nbsp;Bryant will agree
to certain restrictions on the use of confidential information and on solicitation of Intel employees, and will grant a customary release, and Intel will make a separation payment of $4.5&nbsp;million to Ms.&nbsp;Bryant. In addition, Ms.&nbsp;Bryant
is retirement-eligible under Intel&#146;s benefit plans and will receive the retirement benefits described in Intel&#146;s proxy statement for the 2017 Annual Stockholders&#146; Meeting, filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on
April&nbsp;6, 2017. </P>
