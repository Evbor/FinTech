<div style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; TEXT-ALIGN: center">Intel Declares Quarterly Cash Dividend</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; TEXT-ALIGN: left"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif">SANTA CLARA, Calif., March 23, 2017 </font>&#8211;<font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif"> Intel Corporation's board of directors has declared a quarterly dividend of $0.2725 cents per share ($1.09 per share on an annual basis) on the company's common stock. This represents a dividend increase of five percent on an annualized basis. The dividend will be payable on </font><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif">June 1, 2017 to stockholders of record on May 7, 2017.</font></div>
