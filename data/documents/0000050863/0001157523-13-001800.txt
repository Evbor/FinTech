    <p style="text-align: center">
      <font style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Times New Roman"><b>Intel
      Reports First-Quarter Revenue of $12.6 Billion</b></font>
    </p>
    <p>

    </p>
    <p>
      SANTA CLARA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--April 16, 2013--Intel Corporation
      today reported first-quarter revenue of $12.6 billion, operating income
      of $2.5 billion, net income of $2.0 billion and EPS of $0.40. The
      company generated approximately $4.3 billion in cash from operations,
      paid dividends of $1.1 billion, and used $533 million to repurchase 25
      million shares of stock.
    </p>
    <p>
      &#8220;Amidst market softness, Intel performed well in the first quarter and
      I&#8217;m excited about what lies ahead for the company,&#8221; said Paul Otellini,
      Intel president and CEO. &#8220;We shipped our next generation PC
      microprocessors, introduced a new family of products for micro-servers
      and will ship our new tablet and smartphone microprocessors this
      quarter. We are working with our customers to introduce innovative new
      products across multiple operating systems. The transition to 14nm
      technology this year will significantly increase the value provided by
      Intel architecture and process technology for our customers and in the
      marketplace.&#8221;
    </p>
    <p>
      <u>Q1 Key Financial Information and Business Unit Trends</u>
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        PC Client Group revenue of $8.0 billion, down 6.6 percent sequentially
        and down 6.0 percent year-over-year.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Data Center Group revenue of $2.6 billion, down 6.9 percent
        sequentially and up 7.5 percent year-over-year.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Other Intel&#174; Architecture Group revenue of $1.0 billion, down 3.9
        percent sequentially and down 9.0 percent year-over-year.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Gross margin of 56 percent, down 2 percentage points sequentially and
        down 8 percentage points year-over-year.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        R&amp;D plus MG&amp;A spending of $4.5 billion, in line with the company&#8217;s
        expectation of approximately $4.6 billion.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Tax rate of 16 percent.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      <u>Business Outlook</u>
    </p>
    <p>
      Intel&#8217;s Business Outlook does not include the potential impact of any
      business combinations, asset acquisitions, divestitures or other
      investments that may be completed after April 16.
    </p>
    <p>
      <u>Q2 2013</u>
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Revenue: $12.9 billion, plus or minus $500 million.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Gross margin percentage: 58 percent, plus or minus a couple percentage
        points.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        R&amp;D plus MG&amp;A spending: approximately $4.7 billion.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Amortization of acquisition-related intangibles: approximately $70
        million.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Impact of equity investments and interest and other: approximately
        zero.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Depreciation: approximately $1.7 billion.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      <u>Full-Year 2013</u>
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Revenue: low single-digit percentage increase, unchanged from prior
        expectations.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Gross margin percentage: 60 percent, plus or minus a few percentage
        points, unchanged from prior expectations.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        R&amp;D plus MG&amp;A spending: $18.9 billion, plus or minus $200 million,
        unchanged from prior expectations.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Amortization of acquisition-related intangibles: approximately $300
        million, unchanged from prior expectations.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Depreciation: $6.8 billion, plus or minus $100 million, unchanged from
        prior expectations.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Tax Rate: approximately 27 percent for each of the remaining quarters
        of the year.
      </li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 10.0px">
        Full-year capital spending: $12.0 billion, plus or minus $500 million,
        down $1.0 billion from prior expectations.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      For additional information regarding Intel&#8217;s results and Business
      Outlook, please see the CFO commentary at: <u>www.intc.com/results.cfm</u>.
