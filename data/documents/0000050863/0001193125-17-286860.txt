<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Election of New Director </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">On September&nbsp;13, 2017, the Board of Directors (the &#147;Board&#148;) of Intel
Corporation (&#147;Intel&#148;) elected Andrew Wilson as a member of the Board, effective immediately. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">The Board has determined that Mr.&nbsp;Wilson qualifies as
&#147;independent&#148; in accordance with the published listing requirements of NASDAQ. Mr.&nbsp;Wilson has not been appointed to any Board committees at this time. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;Wilson, 43, joined Electronic Arts Inc. (&#147;EA&#148;) in May 2000 and has served as EA&#146;s Chief Executive Officer and director of EA since September
2013. Previously, Mr.&nbsp;Wilson held several leadership positions at EA, including serving as Executive Vice President, EA SPORTS from August 2011 to September 2013. Mr.&nbsp;Wilson also serves as Chairman of the Board of Directors of the World
Surf League. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;Wilson will receive the standard compensation amounts payable to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-employee</FONT> directors of the Board.
Pursuant to these arrangements, commencing in September 2017, Mr.&nbsp;Wilson will be paid an annual retainer of $90,000 (in addition to any committee fees), which will be <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pro-rated</FONT> for his first year of
service. In addition, in the fourth quarter of 2017, Mr.&nbsp;Wilson will be granted <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-employee</FONT> director outperformance restricted stock units and restricted stock units. Mr.&nbsp;Wilson&#146;s awards will
have a cumulative value on the grant date of approximately $146,666, which is <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pro-rated</FONT> from the value of the annual awards granted to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-employee</FONT> directors, and the
awards will vest on the same schedule as the annual awards granted to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-employee</FONT> directors in February 2017, subject to his continued service on the Board. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;Wilson will also enter into Intel&#146;s standard form of directors&#146; indemnification agreement with Intel, pursuant to which Intel agrees to indemnify its
directors to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law and subject to certain conditions to advance expenses in connection with proceedings as described in the indemnification agreement. </P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B>Andrew Wilson Elected to Intel Board of Directors </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">SANTA CLARA, Calif., Sept. 18, 2017 &#150; Intel Corporation today announced that Andrew Wilson, CEO of Electronic Arts Inc., has been elected to Intel&#146;s board of
directors. Wilson&#146;s election brings Intel&#146;s board membership to 12. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">&#147;Andrew understands first-hand how technology and data create opportunity with
