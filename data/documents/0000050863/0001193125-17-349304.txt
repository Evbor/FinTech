<TD WIDTH="12%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">On November&nbsp;15, 2017, the Board of Directors (the &#147;Board&#148;) of Intel Corporation (&#147;Intel&#148;) appointed Kevin T. McBride as Intel&#146;s principal
accounting officer, effective immediately. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;McBride, age 47, is a vice president of finance and serves as corporate controller at Intel.&nbsp;He was
previously Intel&#146;s global accounting and financial services controller from 2013 to 2015 and was promoted to vice president in February 2015.&nbsp;Mr.&nbsp;McBride also served as Intel&#146;s external reporting and treasury accounting
controller from 2010 to 2015 and accounting policy controller from 2005 to 2009, and he has been a fellow at the Financial Accounting Standards Board. Mr.&nbsp;McBride joined Intel in 2000 as an accounting policy manager.&nbsp;He began his career at
KPMG in 1993, last serving as an audit manager. Mr.&nbsp;McBride is a member of the board of directors of Financial Executives International and previously served on the Center for Audit Quality&#146;s SEC Regulations Committee. Mr.&nbsp;McBride
holds a bachelor&#146;s degree in business from Oregon State University and is a certified public accountant in the state of Oregon. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">In connection with his
appointment, Mr.&nbsp;McBride&#146;s salary was increased to $320,000, and his target annual incentive amount under Intel&#146;s Annual Performance Bonus program was set at $316,800. In addition, in connection with his appointment, Mr.&nbsp;McBride
will be granted equity awards in the first quarter of 2018 with a target value on the grant date of approximately $418,900, to be composed of approximately 60% performance-based restricted stock units (OSUs) and 40% time-based restricted stock units
(RSUs). These equity grants will be subject to the terms and conditions of Intel&#146;s 2006 Equity Incentive Plan. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;McBride is not a party to any
transaction with any related person required to be disclosed pursuant to Item 404(a) of Regulation <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">S-K.</FONT> </P>
