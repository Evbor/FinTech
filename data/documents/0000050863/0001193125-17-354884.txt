<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">SANTA CLARA, Calif., Nov. 29, 2017 - Intel announced today the commencement of two related transactions to repurchase three series of its outstanding notes. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B>Exchange Offers </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">The first transaction consists of three
separate private offers to exchange (the &#147;Exchange Offers&#148;) any and all of the outstanding series of notes listed below under the heading Exchange Offers (collectively, the &#147;Old Notes&#148;) in exchange for a combination of newly
issued debt securities of Intel (the &#147;New Notes&#148;) and cash, on the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in the Offering Memorandum dated November&nbsp;29, 2017 (the &#147;Offering Memorandum&#148; and, together with the
accompanying exchange offer notice of guaranteed delivery, the &#147;Exchange Offer Documents&#148;). Only holders who have duly completed and returned an Eligibility Letter certifying that they are either (1) &#147;qualified institutional
buyers&#148; (&#147;QIBs&#148;) as defined in Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the &#147;Securities Act&#148;) or <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">(2)&nbsp;non-&#147;U.S.</FONT> persons&#148; (as defined in Rule 902 under the
Securities Act) located outside of the United States and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-U.S.</FONT> qualified offerees (as defined in the Eligibility Letter) are authorized to receive the Offering Memorandum and to participate in the Exchange
Offers (each an &#147;Exchange Offer Eligible Holder&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">The Exchange Offers will expire at 5:00 p.m. (New York City time) on December&nbsp;5, 2017 (such date
