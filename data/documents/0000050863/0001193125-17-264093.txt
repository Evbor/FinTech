</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:14pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B>Intel Completes Subsequent Offering Period of Tender Offer for Mobileye </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><I>Mobileye Files to Delist from the NYSE </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">SANTA CLARA, Calif.,
and JERUSALEM, August&nbsp;22, 2017 &#150; Intel Corporation (NASDAQ: INTC) and Mobileye N.V. (NYSE: MBLY) today announced the expiration of the subsequent offering period of Intel&#146;s previously announced tender offer to purchase all of the
outstanding ordinary shares of Mobileye. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">The subsequent offering period expired at 11:59 p.m., New York City time, on August&nbsp;21, 2017. Based on information
provided by the American Stock Transfer&nbsp;&amp; Trust Company, LLC, the depositary for the tender offer, a total of 216,757,856&nbsp;Mobileye ordinary shares (including Mobileye ordinary shares tendered during the initial offering period),
