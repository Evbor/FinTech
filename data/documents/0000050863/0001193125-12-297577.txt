ASML research and development (R&amp;D) efforts as well as equity investments in ASML. The first phase of this program consists of Intel committing to R&amp;D funding of &#128;553 million (approximately $680 million) to assist ASML in accelerating
the development and delivery of 450-mm manufacturing tools, as well as an equity investment of &#128;1.7 billion (approximately $2.1 billion) for approximately 10 percent of ASML&#146;s pre-transaction issued shares. Intel will record the R&amp;D
investment as a combination of R&amp;D expense and pre-payments on future tool deliveries. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The second phase of the program is
conditioned upon ASML shareholder approval. It includes an additional commitment by Intel of R&amp;D funding of &#128;276 million (approximately $340 million) in ASML focused on accelerating EUV, as well as an equity investment of &#128;838 million
(approximately $1.0 billion) for an additional 5 percent of ASML post-transaction issued shares. </FONT></P>
