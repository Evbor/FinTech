<td style="font-family:Arial;font-weight:bold;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</td> </tr> </table> <p style="font-family:Arial;font-style:italic;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:6pt">(e) Promotion Equity Award and Compensation Increase </p> <p style="font-family:Arial;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:6pt">On September&#160;10, 2019, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Intel Corporation (&#8220;Intel&#8221;) approved an equity award and cash compensation increase for Todd Underwood in connection with his promotion to Corporate Vice President, Chief Financial Officer of the Client Computing Group. Prior to his promotion, Mr.&#160;Underwood served as Vice President of Finance and Director, Corporate Planning, Reporting and Strategy, and he previously served as Intel&#8217;s interim Chief Financial Officer. </p> <p style="font-family:Arial;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:12pt">Mr.&#160;Underwood will be granted restricted stock units (&#8220;RSUs&#8221;) with a target aggregate grant date value of approximately $1,327,000. The RSUs will vest quarterly over a <span style="white-space:nowrap">3-year</span> period from the grant date and are subject to the terms and conditions of Intel&#8217;s 2006 Equity Incentive Plan. In addition, his total cash compensation opportunity will increase by 16.3%, which includes an increase of his base salary to $400,000 and his annual incentive cash opportunity to $408,000. </p> <div></div> <p style="margin-top:0"></p>
