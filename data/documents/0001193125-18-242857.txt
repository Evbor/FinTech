<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL; " ALIGN="left"><I>Equity Award for Interim Chief Executive Officer </I></P></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">On August&nbsp;2, 2018, the Compensation Committee (the &#147;Committee&#148;) of the Board of Directors of Intel Corporation (&#147;Intel&#148;) approved the grant of
a special equity award to Robert (&#147;Bob&#148;) H. Swan, Intel&#146;s interim Chief Executive Officer (&#147;CEO&#148;) and Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, to reflect his role as interim CEO. The award will have a target
aggregate value on the grant date of approximately $3,270,000 and will consist of approximately 50% time-based restricted stock units (&#147;RSUs&#148;) and 50% outperformance stock units (&#147;OSUs&#148;) by value. The RSUs will vest quarterly
over a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">3-year</FONT> period from the grant date, and the OSUs will vest in February 2021 based on Intel&#146;s performance during the OSUs&#146; performance period. In the event of Mr.&nbsp;Swan&#146;s involuntary
termination without cause or voluntary termination for good reason, the vesting of the RSUs will be eligible for acceleration, and Mr.&nbsp;Swan will be eligible to retain the unvested OSUs. The RSUs and OSUs will be subject to the terms and
conditions of Intel&#146;s 2006 Equity Incentive Plan. </P>
