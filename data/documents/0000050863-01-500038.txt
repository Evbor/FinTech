<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">
<FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2><P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the first quarter, net income, excluding acquisition-related costs, was $1.1 billion, down 64 percent from the first quarter of 2000 and down 58 percent sequentially. First quarter</FONT>

principles because they exclude these costs.</FONT></TD>

<P><FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2>earnings, excluding acquisition-related costs, were $0.16 per share, a decrease of 63 percent from $0.43 in the first quarter of 2000 and down 58 percent sequentially. Last year's first quarter earnings per share
includes a reversal of previously accrued taxes that reduced that quarter's tax provision by $600 million, and improved first quarter 2000 results by $0.09 per share. The reversal was related to the company's previous announcement that the Internal
Revenue Service had closed its examination of the its tax returns up to and including 1998. </FONT></TD>
</TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">
<FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2><P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Including acquisition-related costs, in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles, first quarter net income was $485 million, down 82 percent from first quarter of 2000 and

</TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">
<FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2><P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Acquisition-related costs in the first quarter consisted of $75 million in one-time charges for purchased in-process research and development and $585 million of amortization and
write-offs of goodwill and other acquisition-related intangibles and costs. </FONT></TD>
</TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">
<FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2><P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Our microprocessor business appears to have stabilized and we expect to see normal seasonal patterns going forward from our current business level," said Craig R. Barrett, president and
chief executive officer. "In our communications businesses, we are experiencing continued softness. Looking beyond the current environment, we believe our aggressive investment in new manufacturing technologies and the development of cost-competitive,
leading-edge products is the winning strategy." </FONT></TD>
</TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">
<FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2><P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;During the quarter, the company announced and completed the acquisitions of Xircom Inc. and ICP Vortex Computersysteme GmbH, and announced the acquisition of VxTel Inc. Background on
acquisitions can be found in the first quarter highlights section of this release. </FONT></TD>
</TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">
<FONT FACE="Trebuchet MS" SIZE=2><P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;During the quarter, the company paid its quarterly cash dividend of $0.02 per share. The dividend was paid on March 1, 2001, to stockholders of record on Feb. 7, 2001. Intel has paid a
regular quarterly cash dividend for more than eight years.</FONT></TD>
