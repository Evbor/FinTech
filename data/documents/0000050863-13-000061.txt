<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">As previously announced, effective May 16, 2013, Brian M. Krzanich, became chief executive officer (CEO) of Intel Corporation.&#160;&#160;&#160;On May 16, 2013, the Board of Directors of Intel Corporation elected Mr. Krzanich as a member of the Board and appointed Mr. Krzanich to the Executive Committee of the Board, effective May 17, 2013.&#160;&#160;From 2012 to 2013, Mr. Krzanich was executive vice president and chief operating officer.&#160;&#160;From 2010 to 2012, he was senior vice president and general manager, Manufacturing and Supply Chain.&#160;&#160;From 2006 to 2010, he was vice president and general manager, Assembly and Test.&#160;&#160;Mr. Krzanich joined Intel in 1982. Mr. Krzanich will not receive additional compensation for his Board service.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">At the 2013 Annual Stockholders&#8217; Meeting on May 16, 2013, Paul S. Otellini retired from the board and as president and CEO of Intel. On May 17, 2013, Intel entered into an arrangement with Mr. Otellini to advise the new CEO and other senior management on customer engagements and other management-transition matters.&#160;&#160;The arrangement has a one year term from the date of the 2013 Annual Stockholders&#8217; Meeting, renewable at the request of the CEO and agreement of Mr. Otellini and severable at will by either party.&#160;&#160;Under the terms of the arrangement, Mr. Otellini&#8217;s base pay will be reduced to an annual salary of $100,000 and, upon Compensation Committee approval, he will be granted equity awards in January 2014 with a grant date fair value of $250,000.<font style="FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: inline">&#160;</font>Mr. Otellini&#8217;s existing equity awards are not affected by the arrangement<font style="FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: inline">.&#160;&#160;</font>Mr. Otellini will cease to participate in Intel&#8217;s annual incentive cash plan but will receive a pro-rated payment based on his service through May 16, 2013, and will participate in Intel&#8217;s semi-annual incentive cash plan, with his participation after May 16, 2013 being based on his reduced base pay.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#160;</font><br>
