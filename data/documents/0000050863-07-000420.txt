<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:12pt; margin-left:0.75in;text-align:left;'><font size=2>On May 22, 2007, Intel Corporation (&#147;Intel&#148;), STMicroelectronics NV (&#147;STMicroelectronics&#148;), and certain affiliates of Francisco Partners L.P. (&#147;Francisco Partners&#148; and collectively, the &#147;Parties&#148;) entered into a Master Agreement (the &#147;Master Agreement&#148;) to form a private, independent semiconductor company (&#147;Numonyx&#148;) that will design, develop and manufacture NOR and NAND flash memory products. On December 22, 2007, the Parties entered into a Letter Agreement (&#147;Letter Agreement&#148;) that extends the termination date under the Master Agreement from December 31, 2007 to March 28, 2008.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:12pt; margin-left:0.75in;text-align:left;'><font size=2>In addition, the Parties have re-negotiated the commitment letter with certain lenders which originally stated that the lenders would provide up to $1.55 billion in debt financing for Numonyx. The revised financing terms involve a senior loan of up to $650 million and a $100 million committed revolving credit facility to Numonyx. The financing terms will result in certain changes to&nbsp;the terms of the Master Agreement which are being negotiated by the Parties. The Parties currently plan to close the transactions contemplated by the Master Agreement in the first quarter of 2008.</font></p>
